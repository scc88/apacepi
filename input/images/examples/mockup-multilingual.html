What PAXLOVID is and what it is used for

Sample body · English

PAXLOVID is an antiviral medicine used to treat mild-to-moderate COVID-19 in adults who are at high risk of their illness becoming severe (needing hospital care, or leading to death).

It is a course of two medicines taken together: nirmatrelvir (pink tablet) and ritonavir (white tablet). Start treatment as soon as possible and within 5 days of your symptoms starting. A full course lasts 5 days.

How to take PAXLOVID

Sample body · English

Your dose depends on your kidney function. Always follow your doctor’s instructions.

Dosing by kidney function — a 5-day course
Kidney function (eGFR)Dose
No / mild impairment (≥60)2 nirmatrelvir + 1 ritonavir, twice daily, 5 days
Moderate (≥30 to <60)1 nirmatrelvir + 1 ritonavir, twice daily, 5 days. ⚠ pack may contain more tablets than needed
Severe (<30, incl. dialysis)Day 1: 2 nir + 1 rit once; Days 2–5: 1 nir + 1 rit once daily. On dialysis days, take the dose after haemodialysis. ⚠ pack alert

Take with or without food; swallow tablets whole (do not chew or crush). Missed dose: within 8 hours → take it now and resume your schedule; more than 8 hours → skip it and take the next dose at the usual time. Never take a double dose.

4

Possible side effects

Sample body · English
Commonup to 1 in 10
  • Altered sense of taste
  • Headache
  • Diarrhoea
  • Nausea
Uncommonup to 1 in 100
  • Allergic reactions (itching / skin rash)
  • Increased blood pressure
  • Vomiting
  • Abdominal pain
Rareup to 1 in 1,000
  • Anaphylaxis (severe allergic reaction)
  • Feeling generally unwell
  • Serious skin reaction — Stevens-Johnson syndrome / toxic epidermal necrolysis
Stop and call a doctor now if you have:

trouble swallowing or breathing; swelling of the tongue, mouth or face; throat tightness; hoarseness; itching; or a skin rash (signs of an allergic reaction).

How to store PAXLOVID

Sample body · English

Store at or below 30 °C. Do not refrigerate or freeze. Keep out of the sight and reach of children. Full localised text is generated from the same structured ePI.
