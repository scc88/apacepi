PAXLOVID — clinical-decision-support checks

Live check against the published ePI for TAN, Mei Ling (eGFR 42)

3 alerts
Critical · contraindicated Simvastatin × PAXLOVID

Do not co-administer. Ritonavir strongly inhibits CYP3A4, sharply raising simvastatin levels and the risk of myopathy / rhabdomyolysis. Simvastatin is on the ePI “Do not take with” list.

source: PAXLOVID ePI · PAXH-SIN-0126 · ClinicalUseDefinition (contraindication)

Warning · monitor Warfarin × PAXLOVID

Potential interaction. Monitor INR closely during and shortly after the 5-day course and adjust the warfarin dose as needed; bleeding risk may change.

source: PAXLOVID ePI · PAXH-SIN-0126 · ClinicalUseDefinition (interaction)

Advisory · renal dose Renal dosing — eGFR 42

eGFR 42 mL/min = moderate impairment. The ePI dosing table calls for the reduced regimen.

Recommended: 1 nirmatrelvir (150 mg) + 1 ritonavir (100 mg), twice daily for 5 days. Note: the co-packaged carton may contain more tablets than needed — counsel the patient.

source: PAXLOVID ePI · PAXH-SIN-0126 · dosing by kidney function
